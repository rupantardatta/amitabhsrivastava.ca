About Me

With over [X] years as a dedicated realtor in Mississauga, I've built my career on trust, integrity, and understanding what families in our community truly need. I've served countless residents, helping them find their homes and build their futures here in Ward 7.

As a family man rooted in this community, I understand the challenges middle-class families face—rising property taxes, inadequate public services, and the need for thoughtful development. These aren't just policy issues to me; they're personal.

I'm running for Councillor because I believe our ward deserves representation that listens, acts with transparency, and fights for what matters to you and your family.
