About Me

My experience spans both the private sector and public service, giving me a balanced perspective on how to support residents, strengthen communities, and ensure taxpayer dollars are used wisely.

I am a husband, father of two daughters, entrepreneur, small business owner, businessman, real estate professional, and proud first-generation Canadian.

More than two decades ago, I came to Canada seeking opportunity, safety, and a better future for the family I hoped to build. Like many newcomers, my journey was not always easy. I worked hard, adapted to new challenges, made difficult decisions, and learned firsthand the value of perseverance, community, and opportunity.

Core Values

  • Family First: Decisions that protect our communities
  • Transparency: Open and honest communication
  • Accountability: Following through on commitments
  • Growth: Smart development that preserves character

I was born into a family with a strong tradition of public service, education, and community contribution. My father, who came from a farming background, dedicated his career to public service and was recognized with a Presidential Medal for excellence in duty. Those values of integrity, service, and responsibility have guided me throughout my life and continue to inspire me today.

Over the years, I have built a diverse career that has given me a unique understanding of the challenges and opportunities facing residents. I have worked in retail sales and management, public transit, entrepreneurship, restaurant ownership, and real estate. As an entrepreneur and small business owner, I understand the risks, sacrifices, and determination required to build and sustain a business. As a real estate professional, I work closely with families, homeowners, newcomers, and business owners, helping them navigate some of the most important decisions of their lives.

My education and professional background include Electrical Engineering, immigration consulting, and real estate. These experiences have strengthened my ability to analyze problems, understand complex systems, and develop practical solutions. They have also given me valuable insight into how government policies affect residents, businesses, housing, and the growth of our city.

My wife is a respected leader in public education, and together we have experienced many of the opportunities and challenges faced by families in our community. Raising our two daughters has reinforced the importance of safe neighbourhoods, quality public services, strong community programs, and responsible leadership.

Throughout my career, I have consistently focused on improving systems, simplifying processes, communicating clearly, and finding practical ways to make better use of available resources. I believe government should work the same way—listening to residents, responding to their needs, and delivering value for taxpayers.

I am running for Ward 7 Councillor because I believe our city deserves leadership that is accessible, accountable, and focused on practical solutions. I want to help build a City Hall that listens to residents, spends taxpayer dollars responsibly, and supports strong communities where families, seniors, newcomers, and businesses can thrive.

My Priorities for Ward 7

Residents First

Listening to residents and ensuring their voices are heard at City Hall.

Responsible Spending

Protecting taxpayer dollars and focusing on value for money.

Safe & Well-Maintained Neighbourhoods

Improving traffic safety, community standards, and local infrastructure.

Strong Local Businesses & Jobs

Supporting entrepreneurs, jobs, and economic growth in Ward 7.

Connected Communities

Building stronger relationships between residents, organizations, and City Hall.
